## Why it exists

Cardiac Mass DSS is a prototype for clinical decision support around cardiac mass diagnosis. It explores how decision logic, case data, and traceability can be represented in a structured software system.

## What it does

The project is organized as a TypeScript monorepo with a core package and a frontend package. It is connected to a broader exploration around traceability, decision support, and agentic reasoning.

## Engineering value

The project is valuable as a research prototype. It is less about shipping a polished clinical product and more about understanding how to design systems where reasoning paths, assumptions, and decisions can be inspected.

## What it shows

It belongs in the portfolio because it connects software engineering with a complex domain, where correctness, explainability, and traceability matter more than surface-level UI polish.
